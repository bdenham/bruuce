The **Product Visuals** is powered by Adobe Experience Manager (AEM) Assets and integrated with your Adobe Commerce backend. 

For Storefront Builder, Product Visuals provides the digital access management (DAM) solution for editorial assets like images, videos, and other media used on content pages and product enrichments. The DAM integrates with DA.live and Visual Editor for seamless media content management and works with Edge Delivery Services to deliver optimized assets globally.
