---

The **Product Visuals** is an integration of Adobe Experience Manager Assets with your Adobe Commerce backend. For Storefront Builder, the digital asset management (DAM) solution accesses editorial assets like images, videos, and other media used on content pages and product enrichments. The DAM integrates with DA.live and Visual Editor for seamless media content management and works with Edge Delivery Services to deliver optimized assets globally.
