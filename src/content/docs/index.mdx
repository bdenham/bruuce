Welcome to my personal blog! This site is built using Astro and Starlight, providing a fast and modern platform for sharing content.

## Getting Started

This starter template includes:
- **Blog Section**: Share your thoughts and experiences
- **About Section**: Tell your story
- **Projects Section**: Showcase your work
- **All Starlight Components**: Full access to the powerful component library

Start by adding your content to the respective folders in `src/content/docs/`.
