The Product Discovery drop-in component renders a list of products matching a search query or category with facets used to further refine the list of products shown. The component also supports rendering a search bar and results modal.

With the Product Discovery drop-in component, you can create a seamless and engaging shopping experience for your customers by allowing them to easily find and explore products that match their interests.

## Use cases

The Product Discovery drop-in component provides a variety of fully-customizable controls to showcase your products (according to your brand's aesthetics) and build interactive experiences that engage customers. Use cases include:

- **Use case 1**: Create meaningful search experiences to help shoppers and buyers find what they want with as little effort as possible.
- **Use case 2**: Take advantage of AI-powered dynamic faceting and re-ranking of search results in response to in-session shopper behaviors.
