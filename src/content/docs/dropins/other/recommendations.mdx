The Product Recommendations block is being included as part of Boilerplate and can be configured to use Product Recommendations service.

## Start exploring

The Trending Products block on the product listing pages is rendered by the Product Recommendations.
To learn more about the Product Recommendations, visit [Introduction to Product Recommendations](https://experienceleague.adobe.com/en/docs/commerce/product-recommendations/guide-overview).
