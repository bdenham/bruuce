title: Wishlist overview
description: Learn about the purpose, features, and use cases for the Wishlist drop-in component.
---

The Wishlist drop-in component provides to both guests and registered customers with a mechanism to store
products they are interested in purchasing at a later time. The Wishlist component is fully customizable
and can be integrated into your Adobe Commerce storefront to enhance the shopping experience.

## Use cases

The Wishlist drop-in component provides a variety of fully-customizable controls to showcase your products
(according to your brand's aesthetics) and build interactive experiences that engage customers. Use cases
include:

- **Buy it later**: A user has found an interesting product and wants to keep it in a place that allows to quickly start the purchase process later.
- **Synchronization**: A guest user wishlists a product and once he / she logs in, that product can be seen from other devices in which the user is also logged in.
