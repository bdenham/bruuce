The `OutOfStock` container is designed to handle and display items in the shopping cart that are out of stock or have insufficient quantity. You can configure it to display a message, handle the removal of out-of-stock items, and provide a route to the cart page.

## OutOfStock configurations

The `OutOfStock` container provides the following configuration options:
