The Payment Services drop-in component provides multiple payment options, such as debit card and credit card fields, digital wallets and PayPal products and services to customize the methods available for your store (or multistore) customers.

## Available Payment Services

The Payment Services drop-in component consists of currently one container: `CreditCard`:

- **Credit card container**: The `CreditCard` container is designed to allow placing an order with the available credit card details during the checkout process.
